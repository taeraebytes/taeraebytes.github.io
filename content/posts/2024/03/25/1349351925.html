Fashion anon back - brands do usually give out invites but only for bigger event / international events. E.g when celebs go to pfw you will see them post pics of invites and gifts in a hotel room and thats usually a way to say thanks to the brand because the brand will usually pay for the flight and also pay and set up a hotel room for them with gifts. It’s not as common for domestic events because they don’t get set up in hotels ect the brand might just send and invite or invite and gift if it’s a bigger event, usually contact then for fittings ect but yeah for domestic events it’s not as usual to give invites unless it’s a super large scale event.
